ABOUT SEVENTEEN

SEVENTEEN (세븐틴) is a 13-member South Korean self-producing boy group under Pledis Entertainment, a subsidiary of HYBE Labels. The group consists of S.Coups, Jeonghan, Joshua, Jun, Hoshi, Wonwoo, Woozi, DK, Mingyu, The8, Seungkwan, Vernon, and Dino. They debuted on May 26, 2015, with their first mini album, 17 CARAT.

Known for their impressive performance skills, songwriting, and self-production, SEVENTEEN is divided into three sub-units: the Hip-Hop Unit, Vocal Unit, and Performance Unit. Each member contributes to the group’s creative process, from composing songs to choreographing routines. Over the years, SEVENTEEN has gained international fame with hits like Very Nice, Don’t Wanna Cry, and HOT, earning them multiple awards and recognition as one of K-pop’s most influential acts. Their fandom, called CARATs, passionately supports them worldwide.

SUB UNITS
HIPHOP UNIT

S.Coups, Wonwoo, Mingyu, and Vernon make up the Hip-Hop Unit. They are known for their rap, performance, and stage presence.

PERFORMANCE UNIT

Hoshi, Jun, The8, and Dino form the Performance Unit, excelling in dance and choreography while adding vocal skills.

FUN FACTS

SEVENTEEN is known not only for their powerful performances and self-produced music but also for the unique traits and stories that make them stand out in the K-pop industry. Here are some fun facts that highlight what makes the group special and loved by CARATs around the world:

  • Lucky Number 17: Even though there are 13 members, their name “SEVENTEEN” represents 13 members + 3 sub-units + 1 group = 17.
  • Three Sub-Units: SEVENTEEN is divided into the Hip-Hop Unit, Vocal Unit, and Performance Unit, each highlighting a different talent.
  • CARATs Connection: Their fandom name, CARATs, symbolizes how fans make SEVENTEEN shine brighter, just like a diamond’s carat value.
