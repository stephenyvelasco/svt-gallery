SEVENTEEN GALLERY

Seventeen Gallery is a dedicated showcase celebrating the 13 talented members of the K-pop group SEVENTEEN. The group is divided into three sub-units, each showcasing unique strengths: the Hip-Hop Unit (S.Coups, Wonwoo, Mingyu, Vernon), the Vocal Unit (Joshua, Woozi, Jeonghan, DK, Seungkwan), and the Performance Unit(Hoshi, Jun, The8, Dino). Explore their roles within their sub-units, individual talents, and contributions to the group’s dynamic performances. Fans can enjoy a visual and informative journey through SEVENTEEN’s artistry, connecting with both the members and their music.

ABOUT SEVENTEEN

SEVENTEEN (세븐틴) is a 13-member South Korean self-producing boy group under Pledis Entertainment, a subsidiary of HYBE Labels. The group consists of S.Coups, Jeonghan, Joshua, Jun, Hoshi, Wonwoo, Woozi, DK, Mingyu, The8, Seungkwan, Vernon, and Dino. They debuted on May 26, 2015, with their first mini album, 17 CARAT.

Known for their impressive performance skills, songwriting, and self-production, SEVENTEEN is divided into three sub-units: the Hip-Hop Unit, Vocal Unit, and Performance Unit. Each member to international fame with hits like Very Nice, Don’t Wanna Cry, and HOT, earning them multiple awards and recognition as one of K-pop’s most influential acts. Their fandom, called CARATs, passionately supports them worldwide.

FUN FACTS

SEVENTEEN is known not only for their powerful performances and self-produced music but also for the unique traits and stories that make them stand out in the K-pop industry. Here are some fun facts that highlight what makes the group special and loved by CARATs around the world:

  • Lucky Number 17: Even though there are 13 members, their name “SEVENTEEN” represents 13 members + 3 sub-units + 1 group = 17.
  • Three Sub-Units: SEVENTEEN is divided into the Hip-Hop Unit, Vocal Unit, and Performance Unit, each highlighting a different talent.
  • CARATs Connection: Their fandom name, CARATs, symbolizes how fans make SEVENTEEN shine brighter, just like a diamond’s carat value.
